Description and Responsibilities

As a Success Champion, you’ll help on-board customers, respond to inbound requests, craft informative messages, and build vital relationships.

You are an empathetic communicator who will help our customers learn. You are also interested in analyzing trends and creatively coming up with processes to optimize processes. We’re growing fast and you don’t need to be micromanaged. You thrive with in clear customer success goals and you do exceptional work.
Responsibilities:
• Propose ways to iterate on the customer success program strategy through trials and data analysis
• Provide one-to-one and group coaching to increase product engagement and retention
• Understand all of Better Weekday's feature set and mission
• Support our customers and prospective customers via on-site on-boarding, phone, email, live chat, and webinars
• Identify, propose, and initiate projects that can help our customers be more successful
Required Skills/Experience:
• Outstanding verbal and written communication skills
• Passion for technology
• Interest in design thinking
• Empathetic and patient
• Proactive leadership skills
• Desire to work in an ever-changing, experimental environment
• Demonstrated ability to give and receive feedback and adapt quickly
