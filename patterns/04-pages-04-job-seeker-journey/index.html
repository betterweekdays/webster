Big lists of job titles aren't the most helpful.

New Viewed Degree/Program: Medical Assisting Personalized Internship

MatchRank: 85%, Added 3 days ago

Certified Medical Assistant Medical Receptionist

Indianapolis, IN - Veterans Health Administration

$29,960 median salary


They're looking for: Attention to Detail, Dependability, Cooperation, Integrity

I'll be responsible for: Record patients' medical history, vital statistics, or information such as test results in medical records.
