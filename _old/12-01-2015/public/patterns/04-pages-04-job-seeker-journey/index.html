Your Journey Points: 25

BDubs Bot

Big lists of job titles aren't the most helpful.

Quick summaries of each job lead helps you make a more informed decision.

Job Lead 1 of 11


Email Marketing Manager

San Franciso, CA - Uber Technologies


They're looking for: Experience creating and launching email campaigns using a leading ESP such as ExactTarget and SendGrid.

I'll be responsible for: Developing email marketing strategies that engage millions of users across the globe.

I'll also be responsible for: Set-uping and deploying complex automated communications within ExactTarget and SendGrid

I can expect to make: $59,600 Annual


People who work here say: Super talented people are flocking to Uber from the Bay Area's top tech companies - Things move so much faster here than anywhere else I've worked - you'll be able to get much more done - Legitimately changing the world.

They also say: Long hours (but if they feel like work, you're doing something wrong).

The cost of living is about: $2.80/gal for gas, $1400/mo for rent (900 sq. ft), and $33 to go to dinner for two

I may be promoted to: Senior Marketing Executive

Your Journey Points: 30

BDubs Bot

Each time you engage with a job, I learn more and show you better ones.

Job Lead 2 of 11


Brand Design - Beats by Dr. Dre

Culver City, CA - Apple, Inc.


They're looking for: A student of consumers. Of trends, competitors and culture.

I'll be responsible for: Design and deliver extraordinary brand marketing creative across all virtual and tangible spaces.

I'll also be responsible for: Gather and tell stories that compel consumers to seek out Beats products.

I can expect to make: $56,235


People who work here say: There are limitless advancement opportunities. You work with some very cool people and the leadership cares about your development.

They also say: The pay is excellent but the environment can be very stressful. Management is mostly competent and helpful, but a few bad eggs do make it through.

The cost of living is about: $2.80/gal for gas, $1400/mo for rent (900 sq. ft), and $33 to go to dinner for two

I may be promoted to: Senior Brand Executive
