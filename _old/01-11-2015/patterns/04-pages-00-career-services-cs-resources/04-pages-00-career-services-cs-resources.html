Helpful Student Engagement Tips

What's the best way to follow-up with employers once connected?

Follow up with thank-you notes to employers, preferably a hand written letter or postcard rather than an email.

What is the most important step when establishing employer relationships?

When approaching a potential partner, be sure to explain how the employer will benefit such as making valuable new connections, promoting their brand, and gaining access to potential employees.
